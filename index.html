Jubb Natnapat Kullananant subverts the dominance of semiotic representation by manifesting the presence of materials and trace of labors.

The repetitive folds Kullananant creates on materials, such as paper and glass, engage with their intrinsic properties, leading to irreversible and uncontrollable transformations, revealing new dimensions in perceiving everyday materials.
